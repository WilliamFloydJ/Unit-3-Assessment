Perseverance

I was born in a small home in the suburbs of Sacramento, California, with 4 older brothers. I only lived there for about 3 years before I moved into a nice home in Fort-Worth Texas. It was a rich neighborhood. Lots of middle aged folks, and quite a few kids. Yet I just didn't fit in. I was shy and quite. I grew up only being with my family, but slowly through the years my brothers moved out, leaving me with only my parents. Through my youth I longed to live in Utah, its where 90% of my family lived. Then in 2016 My dad got a job in Salt-Lake. We moved everything, I was happy to start a new, but nervous that i'd have to meat new people. Then 3 days before christmas, my father passed away from a sudden heart attack. That was the hardest time of my life. But really it humbled me. I was a 14 year old kid at the time, and in that short time I had to stand up take charge of my life. I feel like I've aged twice as fast since then. It was only 5 years ago, but It feels like It was an eternity ago. Stay strong, Never forget your worth. Never forget your purpose.
